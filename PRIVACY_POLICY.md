# Privacy Policy for Verasso

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

At Verasso, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our social-learning ecosystem.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Profile Data**: Username, full name, avatar, bio, and interests.
- **Authentication Data**: Email address and MFA (TOTP) status (via Supabase).
- **Service Data**: Messages (End-to-End Encrypted), learning progress, and group memberships.
- **Transaction Data**: Payment history (processed via Razorpay; we do not store full card details).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the Verasso services.
- To facilitate communication and collaboration between users.
- To personalize your learning context via our recommendation engine.
- To ensure security and prevent fraud (via Audit Logs and Device Detection).

## 3. Data Protection (GDPR Compliance)

- **End-to-End Encryption**: Your messages are encrypted using Curve25519; only you and the recipient hold the keys.
- **Right to be Forgotten**: You can delete your account and all associated personal data directly from the app settings.
- **Data Portability**: You may request an export of your personal data by contacting <support@verasso.example.com>.

## 4. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please email us at <privacy@verasso.example.com>.
