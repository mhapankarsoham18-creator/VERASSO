- **Interactive Challenges**: Solve real-world problems through our built-in coding and logic challenges.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Never miss a beat with instant social and system alerts.
- **Privacy First**: Built on modern infrastructure with robust data protection and GDPR compliance.
