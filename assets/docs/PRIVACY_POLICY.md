# Privacy Policy for VERASSO

## Last Updated: February 27, 2026

VERASSO ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Profile Information**: Name, username, bio, and profile picture.
- **Learning Data**: XP earned, badges unlocked, quiz scores, and course progress.
- **Social Data**: Posts, comments, likes, and interactions with other users.
- **Usage Data**: Device information, IP address, and app usage patterns.
- **Device Identifiers**: Push notification tokens (FCM) for system and social alerts.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the VERASSO platform.
- To personalize your learning experience and track gamification progress.
- To facilitate social interactions and community features.
- To send you technical notices, updates, and security alerts.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with:

- **Supabase**: For database hosting and authentication.
- **Sentry**: For error monitoring and performance tracking.
- **Firebase/FCM**: For push notification delivery.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

- **Access**: You can request a copy of your data.
- **Correction**: You can update your profile in the settings.
- **Deletion**: You can delete your account and all associated data via the Settings menu.

## 5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures (Supabase RLS) to protect your data.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: [support@verasso.example.com]
